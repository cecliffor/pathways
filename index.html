Why Choose Pathways Foot & Ankle?

Two things set us apart: We find the root cause of your foot problem with Functional Medicine — and we never let insurance companies dictate your care. Just transparent, affordable, patient-first treatment.

Functional Medicine

We look beyond the foot to find the root cause of your problem — examining nutrition, hormones, inflammation, and lifestyle. Instead of just treating symptoms, we restore whole-body balance to prevent future issues.

Direct Care = Better Care

No insurance middlemen. No surprise bills. Just transparent, affordable pricing and longer appointments. Treatment decisions are made between you and your doctor — not by an insurance company.

Same-Day Visits

No referral needed. Same-day and next-day appointments are usually available. You get direct access to your provider without gatekeepers or prior authorizations.

Conservative First

We start with non-surgical options whenever possible — from orthotics and physical therapy to regenerative medicine and laser therapy. Surgery is always a last resort.

Advanced In-Office Care

X-ray, ultrasound, laser therapy, shockwave therapy, and minor procedures — all in one place. No waiting for outside referrals or separate appointments.

No Surprise Bills

You know exactly what you pay before you receive care. HSA/FSA eligible. Lower overall costs without the overhead of insurance billing.

Craig Clifford, DPM, MHA, FACFAS, FACPM

Dr. Craig Clifford completed his podiatric medical and surgical residency training at the Franciscan Foot and Ankle Institute in Federal Way, Washington, where he later served as Director of Research and then Director of Residency Training. During his teaching tenure, he received multiple honors including Clinician of the Year, Instructor of the Year, and the Outstanding Teaching Award. In 2020, he was named the Washington State Podiatric Medical Association Physician of the Year.


After more than a decade of performing, teaching, and developing surgical techniques, Dr. Clifford recognized that most foot and ankle surgery can be avoided with appropriate conservative care. In 2022, he left his successful high volume surgical practice to focus on expanding patient access to cutting-edge nonsurgical treatment—though he continues to perform surgery when necessary, utilizing the latest advancements in minimally invasive techniques. Dr. Clifford understands that there is no "one size fits all" solution in medicine, and every patient requires an individualized, evidence-based approach, not a generic treatment plan.


Dr. Clifford is board certified by the American Board of Foot and Ankle Surgery and the American Board of Podiatric Medicine. He has previously served on the Board of Directors of the International Foot and Ankle Foundation and has held editorial and peer review roles for multiple medical journals. He was also actively involved for over a decade with the Yucatan Children's Project, where he volunteered his time and skills to perform deformity correction surgeries for underserved children in Mexico.

Medical Services

At Pathways Foot & Ankle, we offer a full range of diagnostic and treatment services — many available right in our office. From advanced imaging to non-surgical therapies and minimally invasive procedures, we tailor every treatment to your condition and lifestyle.

In-Office X-Rays
Get answers fast. Our digital X-ray system allows us to see fractures, bone spurs, arthritis, and alignment issues immediately during your visit — no referral or separate appointment needed.
Diagnostic Ultrasound
This painless, radiation-free imaging lets us examine soft tissues like tendons, ligaments, and nerves in real time. It's especially helpful for diagnosing plantar fasciitis, tendon tears, neuromas, and cysts.
Biomechanical Exam
We analyze how you stand, walk, and run to identify abnormal motion patterns that lead to pain or injury. This exam guides our recommendations for orthotics, physical therapy, and shoe gear.
Functional Medicine Workup and Treatment
Foot problems often stem from whole-body issues like inflammation, hormone imbalances, or nutritional deficiencies. We dig deeper with functional medicine testing to treat the root cause — not just the symptom.
Toenail Removal
For chronic ingrown, thickened, or painful nails, we offer safe in-office nail removal. The procedure is quick, numbed completely, and can be made permanent if the nail edge keeps growing back.
Cortisone Injections
A powerful anti-inflammatory shot that reduces swelling and pain from conditions like arthritis, tendinitis, and plantar fasciitis. Relief often begins within days and can last for weeks or months.
Regenerative Medicine Injections
We use your body's own healing abilities to repair damaged tissues. Options include platelet-rich plasma (PRP) and amniotic injections — ideal for chronic tendonitis, arthritis, and ligament injuries.
Custom Orthotics
Prescription devices made specifically for your feet. Unlike over-the-counter inserts, custom orthotics correct your unique biomechanics, relieve pressure points, and prevent reinjury.
Shockwave Therapy
A non-invasive treatment that uses high-energy sound waves to stimulate healing in chronic conditions like plantar fasciitis and Achilles tendinitis. No needles, no downtime — typically 3 sessions.
Prolotherapy
Prolotherapy (proliferative therapy) uses a small amount of an irritant solution — typically dextrose — injected into a weakened ligament or tendon. This triggers a natural healing response, strengthening the tissue and reducing chronic pain. It's especially effective for ankle instability, plantar fasciitis, and unresolved sprains.
Homeopathic Injection Therapy
A gentle, low-concentration injection that combines diluted homeopathic remedies with a local anesthetic. This therapy is designed to reduce inflammation, break up scar tissue, and relieve nerve pain without the side effects of stronger medications. It's often used for neuromas, arthritic joints, and chronic tendinitis.
Routine Nail and Callus Care
Not just a trim — we medically assess and treat thick, deformed, or fungal nails along with painful, hardened skin. Using sterile instruments, we reduce calluses to prevent ulcers and carefully debride nails to restore comfort and mobility. Ideal for diabetics, seniors, or anyone who struggles to care for their own feet.
Laser Therapy for Pain & Inflammation
Medical grade lasers use focused, painless light energy to penetrate deep into tissues without heat or discomfort. For acute and chronic pain, it reduces inflammation, stimulates cell repair, and improves blood flow — effectively treating arthritis, tendonitis, neuropathy, and sports injuries. Each session takes only a few minutes, and most patients notice improvement after 3–6 treatments.
Laser Therapy for Toenail Fungus
Specialized laser energy safely heats and destroys toenail fungus without damaging the surrounding skin or nail. The treatment is painless, requires no needles or anesthesia, and has no downtime. Most patients see clearer, healthier nail growth after 1–3 sessions, with visible improvement over several months as the nail grows out.
Minimally Invasive Surgery
When surgery is needed, we use small incisions (often less than 1 cm) to correct bunions, hammertoes, neuromas, and more. Less pain, faster recovery, and no large scars.
